About Jordan

- Sports cinematographer -

Jordan has a wide range of experience in video production, including sports and product marketing, daily television, live production, short films and feature stories, but what he loves the most is telling a story.

Whether he's working with a product, a program or a person, Jordan is passionate about finding the story. As a director of photography, Jordan's mission is to deliver images that tell a story and invite viewers to be a part.

Jordan believes that, done well, video has the power to unite, encourage, empower and uplift in a way that perhaps no other medium can.
